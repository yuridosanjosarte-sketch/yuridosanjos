About the Artist

Yuri dos Anjos is a contemporary Indigenous Brazilian artist of Tupi heritage. His work explores ancestry, territory, and spiritual consciousness through oil painting. Each artwork is a unique piece, signed and accompanied by a certificate of authenticity.
